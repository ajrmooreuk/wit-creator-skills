# Engagement Routine — EA for the Rest of Us

## Daily (15 min)

### Block 1: Reply (5 min)
- [ ] Reply to all new comments on latest video
- [ ] Ask a follow-up question in at least 1 reply
- [ ] Heart/like every comment

### Block 2: Outreach (5 min)
- [ ] Leave 3 value-adding comments on competitor channels (rotate weekly):
  1. The EA Podcast
  2. Simon Wardley's channel
  3. TechWithLucy
  4. Lenny's Newsletter (Substack comments)
  5. Ali Abdaal (when topics overlap)

### Block 3: Signal (5 min)
- [ ] Share 1 insight on LinkedIn (primary platform for this audience)
- [ ] Source: latest video key point OR reply to a viewer comment

## Weekly (30 min — Friday)

### Collaborate (15 min)
- [ ] DM 1 creator for potential collab
- [ ] Template: "Hey [name], loved your take on [topic]. I'm covering [related angle] from the SMB perspective — would you be up for a [specific idea: joint video / podcast swap / newsletter mention]?"
- [ ] Update outreach tracker below
- [ ] Follow up on last week's unanswered DMs (once only)

### Listen (15 min)
- [ ] Review this week's comments — note recurring questions
- [ ] Post 1 audience question (LinkedIn poll or YT community tab)
- [ ] Check Analytics → Audience → best publish time
- [ ] Feed top 2 insights back into content-idea-bank-v1.md
